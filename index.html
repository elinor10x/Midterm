Redesigning Montefiore Square

A greener, more comfortable public space

Why this redesign works

I increased tree planting, added planting beds, and replaced hard surfaces with permeable paving. These changes reduce heat, improve drainage, and make the space more inviting.

Key Features

More canopy and shade

Mature trees cool the space and improve comfort.

Permeable paving

Water drains naturally into the ground, reducing runoff.

Community art screen

Solar-powered digital art creates a cultural gathering space.

Flexible seating

Seating, vendors, and structures support social activity.
