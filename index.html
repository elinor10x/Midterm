Redesigning Montefiore Square

A new look for a greener, more enjoyable environment that improves comfort, sustainability, and community use.

See the redesign
What I did

Why this redesign works

I increased tree planting throughout the plaza, added larger integrated planting beds, and replaced sections of hardscape with permeable paving. These changes create more shade, reduce urban heat, improve stormwater handling, and make the space more comfortable and inviting for daily use.

Key features

Small interventions with a big impact

More canopy and shade

Mature, shade-providing trees cool the plaza, improve air quality, and make the area much more comfortable in warm weather.

Permeable paving

Water can move through the ground more naturally, reducing runoff and supporting a more resilient urban landscape.

Community art screen

A digital display powered by solar energy creates a place for public art, culture, and local storytelling.

Flexible social seating

Covered seating, vendor areas, and interactive structures support gathering, rest, play, and local economic activity.

Summary

A greener public space for daily life

The redesigned plaza transforms an underused hardscape into a shaded, sustainable, and multi-functional community destination. By combining environmental improvements with seating, small-business support, and cultural elements, the space becomes more useful, healthier, and more welcoming for residents and visitors.
